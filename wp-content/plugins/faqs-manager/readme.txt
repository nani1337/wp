== Description ==

With <a href="http://www.indianic.com/" target="_blank">IndiaNIC</a> FAQs, you can easily manage frequently asked questions on your website. This assists you with a remarkable feature of adding as many questions and related answers, which in turn, will serve as a forum and may help in bringing a huge traffic to your site. Moreover, there is no knowledge of PHP required; you can simply install the plug-in and you are done.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the 'faqs-manager' folder to the /wp-content/plugins directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Place [iNICfaq id=1] in your post/page where you want the faqs to be displayed.
